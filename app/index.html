Products and Services

Bitcoin Mining

DMG manages a data centre in Edmonton Canada, currently generating more then 4 Petahash. This facility also houses our Bitcoin Mining Education Centre, enabling programming students to learn more about crypto currencies in general, and to experience bitcoin mining in person.

Bitcoin ATMs

DMG manages a network of state of the art Bitcoin ATMs throughout various cities. Our ATMs allow a simple way for consumers to buy & sell Bitcoin using traditional fiat currencies.

DMG Wallet

Our Bitcoin wallet is available to anyone, anywhere in the world. Users can download our DMG wallet app for both IOS and Android smart phones. Simple functionality and ease of use, allows even the most technologically challenged consumers to easily store, transfer and spend bitcoins.

Cloud Mining

DMG is building portable self contained data centres, with each 40 foot shipping container outputting an average of 7.5 Petahash. These units are relocated as continuously lower power costs are sourced. DMG allows consumers to purchase shares of these containers. Please contact us for pricing options.

Retail POS

DMG has developed point of sale software enabling retailers to accept Bitcoin as payment for goods and services. Retailers simply scan QR Code on consumers device and transactions are almost in real time.

DMG Trader

DMG Trader is an app that allows anyone to accept fiat currency, immediately convert to Bitcoin, and transmit to any DMG wallet in the world. The company is exploring various opportunities to enable mass remittance transactions for workers sending money back to their families in such countries including Mexico, Philippines, and China
